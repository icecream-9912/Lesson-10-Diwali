Diwali

Diwali is the festival of Lights. Diwali is celebrated by hindus, sikhs and jains all over the world. Lots of kids, adults and elderly people take part in the fun festivities of diwali as a way to celebrate good over evil.

Activities done on Diwali

  • Making rangolies
  • Lighting sparklers
  • Eating sweets
  • Giving out gifts
  • Lighting fireworks
  • Dancing
  • Meeting family and friends
  • Dressing up nicely
